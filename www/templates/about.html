US Bridges

Discover nearby bridges and explore key information about them with U.S. Bridges.

This app presents data on over 600,000 bridges — from all 50 states, Washington D.C., and Puerto Rico — included in the 2015 National Bridge Inventory (NBI) produced by the U.S. DOT Federal Highway Administration.

This is an open source demo mobile app from @arithmetric. The mobile app is built with the Ionic Framework and Google Maps. The API service is built with Node.js, Restify, and MongoDB.
